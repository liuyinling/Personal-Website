 Short Bio

Yinling LIU has been a postdoc in the SM@RT team of the IRIT CNRS Laboratory since 01/10/2020. His research intere​sts include formal method, domain-specific language, agent-based modeling and simulation, data mining. His current research project involves requirements formalisation and verification, verification of model refinements.
       He did his first postdoc in the DISP laboratory of INSA de Lyon from 01/01/2020 to 30/09/2020. His research project concerned how to create a digital twin of the patient flow process of the Emergency Department in Saint Etienne, France.
      He pursued his Ph.D. in INSA de Lyon ​​​​​from 14/10/2016. During his Ph.D., he studied the research project called "The design and verification of information systems for aircraft maintenance". He successfully defended his Ph.D. thesis on 13/12/2019. Previously, he studied his master in Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics from 2013 to 2016. His master research involved risk analysis of safety-critical systems. Before that he studied his bachelor in Anhui University of Technology from 2019 to 2013.
